PG Classroom CRC Project

Project Overview: The PG Classroom CRC project aims to construct a state-of-the-art classroom for post graduate students.

Location: Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology, Nagpur

Project Duration: 15 months (Expected completion: May 2025)

Key Features:

  • Vibrant Campus
  • 4 Storied Building
  • Air-Conditioned Smart Classrooms

Current Status: The project is currently in the initial construction phase. Foundation work has been completed, and the structural frame is being erected.
